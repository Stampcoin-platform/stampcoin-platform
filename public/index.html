About Stampcoin Platform

What is Stampcoin?

Stampcoin Platform is a blockchain-inspired digital stamps platform featuring:

  • Digital Wallet system for managing balances and stamps
  • Peer-to-peer marketplace for buying and selling collectibles
  • STP token on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20 compatible)
  • Secure transfers and transaction tracking

Technical Details

Token Details:

  • Token Name: StampCoin
  • Symbol: STP
  • Total Supply: 421,000,000
  • Standard: BEP-20
  • Network: BNB Smart Chain (Mainnet)
  • Decimals: 18
